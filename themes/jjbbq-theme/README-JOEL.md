# How to Update Your Website
## Jorkin' Joel's BBQ Hole — Owner's Guide

This guide covers everything you need to manage your site. No coding required.

---

## Adding a New Pop-Up Event

1. Log in to your WordPress dashboard (yoursite.com/wp-admin)
2. In the left menu, click **Pop-Up Events**
3. Click **Add New** at the top
4. Fill in:
   - **Title:** A short label — e.g., "Jukes Ale Works — June 2026"
   - **Event Date:** Pick the date from the calendar
   - **Event Time:** Type the time — e.g., "11am until sell-out"
   - **Venue:** The name of the spot
   - **Address:** Full address
   - **Status:** Set to **Upcoming**
   - **Collaboration Partner:** Optional — e.g., "Julia's Tamales"
   - **Event Flyer:** Upload your flyer image if you have one
5. Click **Publish**

The new event will automatically appear in the "Next Pop-Up" section on your homepage.

---

## Marking an Event as Past or Sold Out

1. Go to **Pop-Up Events** in the left menu
2. Click on the event you want to update
3. Change the **Status** field to **Past** or **Sold Out**
4. Click **Update**

---

## Adding or Hiding a Menu Item

### To add a new item:
1. Go to **Menu Items** in the left menu
2. Click **Add New**
3. Fill in: name, category, price, description
4. Make sure **Show on website menu** is checked
5. Click **Publish**

### To temporarily hide an item (e.g., not available at this event):
1. Open the menu item
2. Uncheck **Show on website menu**
3. Click **Update**

The item stays in your system but won't show on the public menu.

---

## Updating Your Instagram Link (and other site text)

1. In the left menu, click **JJBBQ**
2. Open **Site Settings**
3. Update **Instagram URL**, **Instagram handle**, **Contact email**, **Homepage text**, etc.
4. Click **Save Settings**

For the catering inquiry form: use **JJBBQ → Catering Form** to choose a **Gravity Forms** form (recommended). If none is selected, the built-in form sends email via WordPress.

For the labels on the public **Menu** page tabs: **JJBBQ → Menu Page Tabs**.

---

## Uploading a New Event Flyer

When creating or editing an event, scroll down to the **Event Flyer** field. Click **Add Image**, then either upload a new file or pick one from your media library.
